Walmart Sales Dataset




Dataset Overview


Rows: 6435
Columns: 8

This dataset contains sales data for Walmart, including information about stores, departments, weekly sales, and holidays. Below is a description of the columns:



  • Store: The store number.
  • Date: The week of sales.
  • Weekly_Sales: Sales for the given store and date.
  • Holiday_Flag: Whether the week is a special holiday week (1 = Holiday, 0 = Non-Holiday).
  • Temperature: Average temperature in the region.
  • Fuel_Price: Cost of fuel in the region.
  • CPI: Consumer Price Index.
  • Unemployment: Unemployment rate in the region.
