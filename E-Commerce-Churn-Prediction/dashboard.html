Key Insights from the Dashboard

Tenure-Based Risk

The dashboard reveals that 0-3 month customers exhibit approximately 50% churn rate, making this the most critical intervention window. The risk decreases significantly after 6 months of tenure.

Complaint Correlation

Customers who filed complaints show 31.7% churn rate versus 10.9% for non-complainers - a 2x multiplier effect. This suggests complaint response quality is a critical retention lever.

Satisfaction Paradox

Surprisingly, customers with satisfaction scores of 3-4 (mid-range) show higher churn than expected. This "hidden risk" segment requires deep-dive surveys to understand their silent dissatisfaction.

Cashback Engagement

High-value customers with low cashback usage represent untapped retention opportunities. Dynamic cashback targeting could increase engagement by 2x in this segment.

Device Behavior

Mobile-first customers spend 40% more time on app but show varied churn patterns based on feature usage. App experience optimization is key for this segment.

Order Value Trends

Customers with declining order values (negative year-over-year growth) are 3x more likely to churn. Early warning systems can flag this behavioral shift.

Customer Segmentation Strategy

Based on the dashboard analysis, customers can be grouped into three strategic segments

HIGH RISK

The Frustrated Newcomer

Churn Rate ~50%
Population ~15%

Profile:

  • Tenure: 0-3 months
  • Has filed complaints
  • Low app engagement
  • Quick to churn

Immediate Intervention:

  • 24-hour complaint response guarantee
  • Proactive onboarding support
  • VIP escalation path
  • Satisfaction check-ins at days 7, 30, 60
MEDIUM RISK

The Quietly Disengaged

Churn Rate ~25%
Population ~30%

Profile:

  • Low cashback usage
  • Increasing days since last order
  • Declining purchase frequency
  • No formal complaints

Re-engagement Campaign:

  • Personalized win-back offers
  • Dynamic cashback incentives
  • Product recommendation refresh
  • Email nurture sequence
HIDDEN RISK

The Confidently Dissatisfied

Churn Rate ~20%
Population ~25%

Profile:

  • Satisfaction score: 3-4/5
  • No formal complaints
  • Stable but not enthusiastic
  • "Silent churners"

Deep Dive Investigation:

  • Follow-up surveys to uncover issues
  • Qualitative research interviews
  • A/B test experience improvements
  • Loyalty program enhancements
